Two Innovation Tracks

Whether you're technical or non-technical, there's a track for you to showcase your genius

Functional Track

Focus on GenAI applications that solve business problems and improve customer-facing or operational processes. Build solutions that end users directly interact with to accomplish business objectives.

  • Customer service automation
  • Business intelligence assistants
  • Document processing solutions
  • Personalized user experiences

Technical Track

Focus on GenAI applications that enhance the software development lifecycle and improve internal productivity. Build solutions that help developers, testers, and technical teams work more efficiently.

  • Code review assistants
  • QA framework automation
  • Requirements to code translation
  • Development process optimization

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about participating

Who can participate in Hack the Genius?

All Myridius employees globally can participate, regardless of technical background. We welcome both technical and non-technical team members.

Do I need coding experience to participate?

Not at all! Teams can create solutions using no-code platforms like Retool, N8n, Microsoft Power Platform, Bubble, Glide, or Chatbase.

What's the team size and composition?

Teams must have exactly 3 members. You can mix technical and non-technical backgrounds. Cross-regional teams are encouraged but not mandatory. If you register individually, our panel will help form teams.

What's the registration deadline?

September 8, 2025, by end of day. No extensions will be provided, so make sure to submit your registration on time.

What information do I need for registration?

You'll need: Team name & theme selection, Problem statement (200 words), Proposed solution approach (300 words), Team member profiles (name, role, location), and Expected outcomes & impact.

How will solutions be evaluated?

Primary criteria (70%): Use of AI tools & innovation (25%), Technical implementation (25%), Business impact (20%). Secondary criteria (30%): Presentation quality (15%), Team collaboration (10%), Future scalability (5%).

What are the prizes and recognition?

Grand prizes for overall winner and track winners, special recognition for Most Innovative Solution and Best Cross-Regional Team. All participants receive certificates and Amazon vouchers.

What platforms and tools can we use?

Any combination of cloud computing (AWS/Azure/GCP), GenAI platforms (OpenAI, Claude, Gemini), development stacks, and no-code platforms. You can also use additional platforms like OpenLLaMA, crewai, langflow, Salesforce, ServiceNow.

When do demo videos need to be submitted?

September 19, 2025. Videos should be exactly 5 minutes long showcasing your solution, explaining the problem it solves, and demonstrating its key features.

How will final presentations work?

Top 10 teams will present live demos to the jury panel on September 26-27. You'll have time for a presentation followed by Q&A from the judges.

What happens to winning solutions?

Winning solutions will be considered for integration with Evoq offerings, client presentations, and potential funding for go-to-market development. There's also opportunity for IP protection and mentorship.

Can I change my team composition after registration?

Minor changes may be accommodated before September 8. Contact the support team through the dedicated Teams channel for assistance with team modifications.

Are there any restrictions on external APIs or services?

No major restrictions. Teams can use external APIs, services, and integrate with existing systems as needed for their solution. Just ensure you have proper access and permissions.

Where can I get help and support?

Use our dedicated Teams channel for fastest response, email hackthegenius@myridius.com for general inquiries, or contact your regional PoCs for location-specific support.

What if I have more questions during the hackathon?

We'll have a Global Call on September 15 to provide details about submissions and answer questions. You can also reach out anytime through our support channels.
